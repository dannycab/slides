# CERL Group Health & Coordination
## Planning for Our Growth Together

---

### Why We Are Having This Discussion

* Our group is growing quickly and developing numerous threads of work.
* This is a natural result of a thriving, expanding community!
* **Our Goal:** Use this time to proactively plan, build, and adapt our systems so that we keep doing great work without anyone becoming overburdened.
* *Note: This is an ongoing process and just the start of our planning together.*

---

### Overview: Section 1 (Shared Expectations)

* **Semesterly Reviews:** Keeping our group norms dynamic and up to date.
* **Living Resources:** Centralized wiki and onboarding materials?
* **Collective Accountability:** Safe pathways to address issues and any overburdening early.

# Deep Dive: Section 2
## Redesigning Group Service

---

### Rethinking How We Distribute Work

* Service work shouldn't just fall on whoever happens to say yes.
* Workload should scale intentionally with seniority and other responsibilities.
* **Visible & Recognized:** Documenting all recurring tasks and ensuring they are formally credited.
* **Indentifying Gaps:** Regularly checking in to see if any roles are being neglected or over-concentrated.

---

### Core Service Roles to Document

We want to map out, organize, and distribute roles like:
* **Facilitation:** Running meetings and setting agendas.
* **Note-Taking:** Documenting decisions and action items.
* **Onboarding Buddies:** Helping newer members settle in.
* **Technical Support:** Maintaining shared lab resources and drives.
* **Social Coordination:** Organizing group events and check-ins.
* **Anything Else?** Let's brainstorm together!
