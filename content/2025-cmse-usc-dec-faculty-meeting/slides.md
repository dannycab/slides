# Why CMSE 101?

- AI/ML is transforming society—MSU must lead in **AI literacy for all students**.
- CMSE is uniquely positioned:
    - **Interdisciplinary expertise** in computation and pedagogy
    - Proven success with **large-scale, evidence-based courses**
- Goal: Equip students with **conceptual and critical understanding** of AI.

---

# What is CMSE 101?

- **3-credit general education course** on AI/ML and data science.
- Accessible to **all majors**—no programming prerequisite.
- Modular design:
    - Interactive case studies
    - GUI-based programming
    - Critical media analysis
- Anchored by **DTPA Framework**: Data, Tools, Practices, Actions.

---

# Timeline & Impact

- **Pilot:** Fall 2026 → Scale-up through 2029.
- Aligns with MSU’s **Gen Ed Modernization**.
- Positions CMSE as a **national leader in AI education**.
- Long-term: Reach **thousands of students annually**, sustainable revenue.
- Funding request: **$150K/year for 3 years** for development & instructional support.
