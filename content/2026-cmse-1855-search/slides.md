**Diversity** - The active and intentional effort to broaden the representation of different identities and experiences within the MSU community. This includes reducing bias in programs and processes.  

---

## 1855 Search – AI in STEM Education  

**Equity** - The creation of opportunities, programs, and policies that ensure all individuals have access to the resources and opportunities they need to succeed. This includes removing barriers that have historically and contemporarily disenfranchised individuals in a field.  

**Inclusion** - The active, intentional, and ongoing effort to ensure that diverse individuals are fully integrated into all aspects of the work and learning environment. This includes promoting a sense of belonging for all individuals.

## A Comment on Publications

Much like other fields, in Education Research, there are tiers of publications:

1. **Peer-reviewed Journal Articles** - longer articles (12-50+ pages); usual review processes. Highly valued across sub-fields. (*e.g., Computers and Education, International Journal of STEM Education*)
2. **Peer-reviewed Conference Proceedings** - shorter articles (2-6 pages); some form of review. Highly valued in some fields; varied quality and impact (*e.g., ACM Conference Proceedings*)
3. **Peer-reviewed Conference Abstracts/Presentations** - quite short writing (500-2000 words); peer reviewed often to allow author to present. Highly valued in some fields; varied quality and impact.

## How can we make this decision?

### First, consider your impressions and biases (10 minutes)

- Take 5 minutes and just talk to the people around you about the candidates and your views of them, their records, and so on.
- **Pause for 2 minutes (silently)** and consider your implicit biases. *Remember those biases can be negative and positive and that we are all biased.*

**Scoring summaries (0/1/2) by criterion:**  
- **A (Inclusion):** 2 = clear understanding + track record; 1 = past commitment + plans; 0 = limited appreciation/no plans. 
- **B (Research):** 2 = strong/steady record + clear pathway; 1 = adequate record + clear direction; 0 = weak/incremental.

**Criteria (Second‑Pass) & 0–3 anchors:**
